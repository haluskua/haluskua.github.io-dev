Padding - Margin - Border

So the difference between Pargin and Margin is that while padding deals with the inner space, margin deals with the outer space to the next outer element. Padding is space inside the Border, whereas Margin is space outside the border. {taken from google-search}

The way I see it is: Say the 'content' is your house in the forest. The padding is the space between your house and the Fence around your house. Ofcourse that will mean the Border is your Fence. Lastly the space from the fence to the tree-line or forest is the Margin which is the end of the line or perimeter. Yet do not forget that not all houses have margins or borders or paddings. It depends on the designer.

The picture below shows how it is applied in a test environment. I tend to use margin more ofen as it can affect other elements around or near it which are more or less touching each others boundaries.
